How to Access

1. Join the Spurs Discord Server.

2. Open the Activities tab in any voice channel.

3. Search for and launch DisCards.

The Binder System

Your Collection Hub

The Binder is where all your collected cards are stored. It acts as your personal inventory, allowing you to view your progress, check card stats, and manage your full roster.

Manage Upgrades

You can access the Upgrade System directly from the Binder. If you have enough duplicates, you can merge them here to elevate your cards to the next tier.

The 6 Houses

Cards are divided into six distinct houses. Collecting players from the same house is key to winning games.

Chemistry & Performance

The Chemistry Advantage

Rating isn't everything. A lower-rated team with high chemistry (matching houses and positions) has a strong chance to outperform a higher-rated team made of random players.

Server Rank & Card Types

Depending on your current rank within the server, your cards can feature different stats, positions, and abilities.
